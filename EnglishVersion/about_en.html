About Us

Welcome to the ShinyLeo Tribe Maine Coon cattery. Our cattery is located in Bataysk (Rostov Region). My name is Svetlana Stribnaya, and I am the owner of this cattery.

Our cattery is part of our family. All Maine Coons live with us at home, surrounded by care, attention, and love. We pay special attention to the health and socialization of our kittens, as well as their compliance with breed standards.

Maine Coons are remarkable cats with a gentle temperament, friendly nature, and excellent compatibility with children and other pets. Their affectionate “singing” and regal appearance make them true treasures in any home.

Cattery Opening Certificate

Our cattery has been officially registered since 2020.

Show Participation

Our Maine Coons regularly participate in exhibitions and win prizes, confirming their high quality and adherence to breed standards. We approach each preparation with love and take pride in our pets’ achievements.
